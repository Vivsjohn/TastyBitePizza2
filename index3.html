HISTORY
Tasty Bite Pizza Business was founded on September 26, 2025. It was established in Poblacion, Boljoon, Cebu. Tasty Bite Pizza Business was built because of the increased population of the people in the community. Tasty Bite Pizza Business has affordable products so that the people in the community can afford them.
LIFE OF THE FOUNDER
Ms. Apple Eve Roma Rosos is the owner of Tasty Bite Pizza Business in Poblacion, Boljoon, Cebu. She is an only child who is independent and hardworking, managing her business on her own. Through determination and self-reliance, she was able to establish her own source of income and continues to run the business responsibly to support her present needs and future plans.
THE SUCCESS STORY OF THE
BUSINESS
The business started in a small space with limited resources. Through the dedication, consistency, and hard work of the workers, the business continued to grow and improve. As time passed, the increase in customers and products led to the expansion of the business space. With a larger area, the business was able to offer more products and better serve the needs of the community.
THE CHALLENGES MEET BY
THE FOUNDER
Ms. Apple Eve Roma Rosos also faced challenges running Tasty Bite Pizza Business, such as competition from nearby stores, unpredictable customer behavior, and balancing business operations with personal responsibilities. As the business grew, managing orders, supplies, and customer demands became more demanding. Despite these difficulties, with careful planning, persistence, and support from friends and family, she was able to overcome these obstacles and keep the business running smoothly.
HOW WILL YOU SEE YOUR BUSINESS 10 YEARS
AFTER
In 10 years, I see Tasty Bite Pizza Business as a successful and well-managed small business. It will be financially stable, offering a wider variety of quality pizzas and other products that meet the needs of the community. The business will provide steady income and possibly create opportunities for others to work or collaborate, helping families in the area become more self-reliant. Through continuous improvement, dedication, and strong management, Tasty Bite Pizza Business can serve as an example of how hard work and commitment can grow a small enterprise and support the community.
